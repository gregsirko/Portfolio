How I Got Here

By Design?

Things pop out at me and they don’t shut up: a pixel out of place, a misspelled word, or mismatched shades of black. Code doesn’t run with missing keystrokes.

Laser-focused precision and a sensitivity to what feels natural allow me to deliver robust experiences that flow elegantly and fit the user’s needs, rather than the contrary.
